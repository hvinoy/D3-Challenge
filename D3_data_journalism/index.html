Correlations Discovered Between Poverty and Healthcare

This chart shows data about the poverty percentage and lack of healthcare percentage in the United States. As indicated by the data points in the scatter chart, lower poverty percentage levels correlates to lower lack of healthcare percentage in that state. Sates with higher poverty levels are correlated to having a higher lack of healthcare for the population in that state. This could be caused by the inability of the population with state's that have a higher poverty rate to afford proper healthcare coverage.
